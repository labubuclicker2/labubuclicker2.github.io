About Labubu Clicker

Let’s dive into the amazing world filled with tons of adorable Labubu. Click repeatedly and invest the earned points in handy upgrades that help speed up your point generation dramatically. Play Labubu Clicker on GitHub now, and collect and unbox fantastic blind boxes to meet beautiful Labubus.

Labubu Clicker Gameplay Breakdown

Labubu Clicker is a fun, super engaging idle clicker game where you aim to collect as many blind boxes as possible through clicking and buying upgrades. You will get a brand new secret box when you collect enough points to 100% fill up the progress bar. Each box contains a secret Labubu that is added to your huge Labubu collection once you unbox it.

There are a total of 18 upgrade choices included in the game, divided into two major categories: power clickers and auto-clickers. Once you have enough Labubu points (by clicking to generate), buy upgrades to gain points more quickly and efficiently.

How to Play Labubu Clicker: Explained

You start the game with a given blind box. Tap or click on it to reveal your very first Labulu, which will be with you in the first stage of the game.

Generate the first points by clicking or tapping on the screen

One click yields one point, and the more you click, the more points you collect. Greatly, if you click fast enough, you will double your click points.

Purchase upgrades

Use your earned points to buy early upgrades, such as Cursor or Auto Click, which give you some initial boosts. When you have more points and unlock more upgrade options, buy powerful ones to skyrocket your point earnings.

Collect blind boxes and get your Labubus

Anytime the bar beneath your Labubu is filled up, you will get a box with a secret Labubu inside; well, you do not know how it looks and how cute it is until you click to unbox it. Focus on earning more and more points through your clicks and upgrades so you will have more and more boxes to unbox (with more Labubus to add to your collection).

Catch flying Labubu

 Sometimes, you will see a small, golden Labubu flying from the left of the screen. Don’t ignore them since those Labubu can give you a dramatic boost, depending on your current score. So, click to catch them as quickly as you can.
